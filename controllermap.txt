5U - Ports 5&5 go reverse, ports 7&8 forward | arm lower
6D - Port 2 forward |conveyor forward(toggle)

Joystick Axis 1- - port 3 reverse port 4 forward | launcher speed (adjustable) or arm move (adjustable)
Joystick Axis 2+ and 2- - unmapped
8R - Port 1 Reverse | transmission move
8L - Port 1 Forward | transmission move
Joystick Axis 3+ - Port 9 forward, Port 10 reverse
Joystick Axis 3- - Port 9 reverse, Port 10 forward
Joystick Axis 4+ - Ports 9 & 10 forward
Joystick Axis 4- - Ports 9 & 10 reverse
